Hashup Inc. Co-Founder, CTO | 株式会社Hashup 代表取締役 兼 CTO

I am the CTO of Hashup Inc., where I lead AI-driven product development, committed to creating impactful solutions that merge cutting-edge technology with practical applications. With a solid foundation in machine learning and product development, I offer consulting and development services that help organizations leverage AI for innovation.

In my previous role as a researcher at Nagoya University, I focused on advancing fields such as Wearable Devices, Ubiquitous Computing, Human-Computer Interaction, IoT, and Digital Textiles. My expertise spans a range of data analysis techniques—time series processing, 2D pressure data, image recognition, and machine learning—to support groundbreaking projects. I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of technology to solve real-world challenges.
